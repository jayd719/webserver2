Customization Options

Stats Card Options

  • ?theme=light or ?theme=dark
  • &font=FontName (e.g., Karma, Abel, etc.)
  • &ext=heatmap - adds activity heatmap
  • &border=border_color - adds border

Badge Options

Format: https://badges.peiyuan.ch/leetcode/jashansingh29/type?param=value

  • Types: ranking, solved, acceptance, streak
  • Parameters:
    • logo=leetcode - shows LeetCode logo
    • color=color_name - blue, green, yellow, red, etc.
    • style=flat - changes badge style
