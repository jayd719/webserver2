About Me

Hi, I'm Jashandeep Singh, a passionate and innovative engineering professional with over five years of experience at the exciting crossroads of manufacturing and software engineering. My journey has been defined by a love for learning, a commitment to solving complex problems, and a deep curiosity about how technology can transform traditional workflows into efficient, streamlined processes.

I specialize in CNC programming, CAD design, process optimization, and lights-out machining, skills that have allowed me to deliver meaningful results throughout my career. My unique ability to combine hands-on engineering expertise with advanced software knowledge has empowered me to bridge the gap between two seemingly distinct fields: manufacturing and technology.

I started my career immersed in the world of manufacturing, where I honed my technical skills and built a strong foundation in engineering principles. Working at Canadian Babbitt Bearings, I quickly developed a knack for process optimization and identifying opportunities for efficiency. As I gained experience, I became fascinated by the role of technology in improving manufacturing workflows and began exploring ways to integrate software into traditional engineering practices.

One of the standout moments in my early career was designing custom CNC programs that standardized operations, reduced errors, and significantly improved efficiency. This achievement not only sharpened my technical expertise but also inspired me to look for innovative solutions beyond conventional approaches.

Over time, I discovered that the intersection of manufacturing and technology was where my true passion lay. I began to explore how software solutions could drive efficiency in manufacturing processes, and this interest led to some of my most impactful projects.

One of these projects was the development of a custom data-processing application for a CRM system. The application automated the generation of dashboards, reports, and alerts, significantly reducing manual data entry and analysis. By providing real-time insights, the tool empowered my team to make faster, data-driven decisions, ultimately improving operational efficiency. Seeing the transformative impact of this solution solidified my belief in the power of technology to revolutionize traditional workflows.

Another highlight was implementing lights-out machining on the Matsuura H-Plus 630, an initiative that increased production capacity by an impressive 40%. By rethinking machine utilization and leveraging after-hours production, I helped unlock untapped potential in existing resources. This project exemplified my ability to combine strategic planning with technical execution to deliver tangible results.
